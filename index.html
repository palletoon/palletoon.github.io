Responsibilities

TESOL teachers, aka ESL (English as a Second Language) teacehrs, teach English language lessons to non-native and native speakers. They usually work in schools or other educational settings.

  • c

    Lesson Planning

    Developing lesson plans according to SOL and WIDA standards

  • b

    Delievery

    Teach the lesson to a classroom (push-in or pull-out) of students

  • d

    Engagement

    Effectively maintain the interest of students to maximize learning

  • f

    Differentiation

    Differentiate instruction to meet all students' learning needs

  • p

    Assessment

    Gathering and synthesizing information in regards to student learning

  • m

    Washback

    Provide relevant and timely feedback on achievement--performance and growth and developmental needs to student
